# Office Time Tracker 🕐

A colorful, single-file HTML5 app to track your office hours. Enter your swipe-in time, set your required hours goal, and get notified the moment you're free to leave.

## Features

- **Live clock** — displays current time in 12-hour format
- **Flexible hour goals** — choose 4, 6, 8, or 9 hours (half day to long day)
- **Swipe-in time entry** — manual entry or one-click "Now" button
- **Live progress tracking** — elapsed time, remaining time, and animated progress bar
- **Milestone markers** — visual highlights at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100%
- **Completion alert** — on-screen message, chime sound, and blink animation when goal is reached
- **Reset** — clears all state to start a new session

## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser — no server or install needed.
2. Select your required hours (4 / 6 / 8 / 9).
3. Enter your swipe-in time or click **Now**.
4. Click **Start tracking**.
5. Watch the progress bar fill up. You'll be alerted when your hours are complete.
6. Click **Reset tracker** to start over.
